07 — Next quarter

What we do with this.

Six moves that follow directly from the numbers above — designed to protect what worked and fix what didn't.

1

Arrest the monthly decline

GMV fell each month (Apr → May → June). Diagnose the driver — fewer new videos, seasonality, or hero fatigue — and make a reactivation push the top Q3 priority.

2

Double down on HMB Muscle Shield

One SKU drove ~92% of GMV. Keep it the lead in every brief, and test whether Hair Health+ can grow into a reliable second engine.

3

Re-sign the top performers

philsmypharmacist and the April–May leaders drove outsized share. Lock repeat content with them before competitors do.

4

Diversify beyond one product

92% of GMV in a single SKU is a concentration risk. Seed secondary SKUs (Multivitamin, Biotin) with proven creators to broaden the base.

5

Recruit Spanish creators

Only 4 of 295 converting videos were Spanish — essentially untapped. A small, targeted Spanish push is low-cost, high-ceiling.

6

Reconcile to Seller Center

These figures are video-attributed from Euka. Pull the Seller Center creator and video lists to fold in LIVE and product-card sales and confirm official totals.
